Refund Policy

Last Updated: March 15, 2025

1. Overview

At Salt & Frame, we are committed to providing exceptional contemporary photography and visual branding services. This Refund Policy outlines the circumstances under which refunds may be granted for our services. This policy should be read in conjunction with our Terms & Conditions.

Salt & Frame operates from Apt 12 / 45 Sandpiper Street Casuarina NT 0810 Australia (ABN: 39735844). We comply with Australian Consumer Law, which provides protections and guarantees for consumers.

2. General Refund Principles

Due to the custom and creative nature of our photography and branding services, refunds are evaluated on a case-by-case basis. Our refund policy takes into account:

  • The stage of project completion when refund is requested
  • The amount of work already completed
  • Resources allocated to your project
  • Time blocked in our schedule that could have been allocated to other clients
  • Whether services meet the standards guaranteed under Australian Consumer Law

3. Deposit Refunds

3.1 Before Work Commences

If you request a cancellation before any work has begun on your project:

  • More than 30 days before scheduled session/start date: Full refund minus 10% administration fee (AUD $50 minimum)
  • 15-30 days before: 50% refund of deposit paid
  • Less than 15 days before: No refund; deposit is forfeited

The administration fee covers initial consultation time, scheduling coordination, and administrative processing.

3.2 After Work Has Commenced

Once work on your project has begun, including:

  • Photography session conducted (even partially)
  • Design concepts created
  • Research or planning specific to your project
  • Materials purchased for your project

Deposits are non-refundable. You remain liable for the full contracted amount. We may, at our discretion, offer partial credit toward future services.

4. Cancellation Timeframes

4.1 Photography Sessions

For individual photography sessions:

  • 30+ days notice: Full refund minus 10% administration fee
  • 15-30 days notice: 50% refund
  • 7-14 days notice: 25% refund
  • Less than 7 days notice: No refund

4.2 Multi-Day or Package Services

For comprehensive branding projects or multi-session packages:

  • 30+ days before project start: Full refund minus 20% administration fee
  • 15-30 days before: 40% refund
  • Less than 15 days before: No refund

If project is partially completed, refunds are calculated based on work completed versus total project scope.

5. Rescheduling vs. Cancellation

We understand that circumstances change. To avoid cancellation penalties:

  • First Reschedule: Complimentary if requested at least 14 days in advance
  • Additional Reschedules: AUD $150 fee per occurrence
  • Less than 7 days notice: Treated as cancellation under Section 4

Rescheduling is subject to availability and must be completed within 6 months of original booking date.

6. Weather-Related Cancellations

For outdoor or location-based photography sessions affected by weather:

  • If we determine weather conditions are unsuitable, rescheduling is offered at no charge
  • No refunds are provided for weather-related rescheduling
  • If rescheduling is not possible within a reasonable timeframe, a full refund will be provided
  • Client-initiated cancellations due to weather predictions follow standard cancellation terms

7. Dissatisfaction with Services

7.1 Quality Concerns

If you are dissatisfied with the quality of our work:

  • Notify us in writing within 7 days of receiving deliverables
  • Provide specific details about quality concerns
  • We will review concerns and offer corrections or re-shoots where appropriate

Subjective preferences ("I don't like the style" or "It's not what I imagined") are not grounds for refunds if work was completed according to agreed specifications.

7.2 Service Failures

Refunds may be granted if we fail to:

  • Deliver services with due care and skill
  • Provide services fit for stated purpose
  • Meet agreed specifications outlined in service agreement
  • Deliver within reasonable timeframes (unless delays are client-caused)

These rights are protected under Australian Consumer Law and cannot be excluded.

8. Partial Refunds for Incomplete Projects

If a project is terminated before completion by either party:

  • Payment is due for all work completed to date
  • This includes concept development, photography sessions conducted, and design work completed
  • Work is charged at the hourly rate specified in the service agreement or AUD $200/hour if not specified
  • Any amounts paid beyond work completed will be refunded within 14 business days

9. Force Majeure and Emergency Situations

9.1 Cancellation by Salt & Frame

If we must cancel due to circumstances beyond our control, including:

  • Serious illness or emergency affecting key personnel
  • Natural disasters or extreme weather
  • Government restrictions or mandates
  • Equipment failure despite reasonable maintenance

You will receive the option of:

  • Full refund of all amounts paid, or
  • Rescheduling to a mutually convenient date

9.2 Client Emergency Situations

In documented emergency situations (serious illness, death in family, natural disasters affecting client), we will work with you to:

  • Reschedule without penalty, or
  • Provide refunds exceeding standard policy where appropriate

Documentation may be required for emergency exception requests.

10. Delivery and Completion

10.1 Post-Delivery Refunds

Once final deliverables have been provided and accepted:

  • No refunds are available
  • Copyright and usage rights have been granted as per agreement
  • Payment obligations are complete

Acceptance is assumed if you:

  • Use deliverables publicly or commercially
  • Do not raise concerns within 7 days of delivery
  • Request final deliverables be released

10.2 Revision Requests After Delivery

Revision requests after final delivery may incur additional fees. Refunds are not provided because you later decide to request changes beyond the agreed revision rounds included in your service package.

11. Digital Products and Downloads

For digital deliverables including:

  • High-resolution image files
  • Logo and branding files
  • Design templates or assets

Once you have downloaded or received access to digital files, no refunds are available. Digital products cannot be "returned" in the traditional sense. Ensure you review preview versions or proofs before requesting final files.

12. Refund Processing

12.1 Request Process

To request a refund:

  1. Contact us by phone (+61889456723) or via our contact page
  2. Provide your booking reference or service agreement number
  3. Explain the reason for your refund request
  4. Provide any relevant documentation

We will acknowledge your request within 2 business days and provide a decision within 7 business days.

12.2 Refund Timeline

Approved refunds will be processed:

  • Within 14 business days of approval
  • To the original payment method when possible
  • Bank transfers may be used for large amounts or when original method is unavailable

12.3 Refund Method

Refunds are issued via the original payment method unless:

  • The original method is no longer available
  • You request an alternative method in writing
  • Bank transfer is more appropriate for the amount

13. Non-Refundable Items and Services

The following are non-refundable under all circumstances:

  • Consultation fees for initial project discussions
  • Travel expenses incurred for location shoots
  • Third-party costs (location permits, specialized equipment rental, etc.)
  • Rush service fees
  • Custom materials or products purchased specifically for your project
  • Services fully completed and accepted

14. Disputes and Australian Consumer Law

14.1 Your Rights

This Refund Policy does not limit your rights under Australian Consumer Law. You are entitled to:

  • A refund or replacement if services are not provided with acceptable care and skill
  • Compensation for reasonably foreseeable loss or damage
  • A refund if services are not fit for the purpose you specified
  • Services completed within a reasonable time

14.2 Dispute Resolution

If you disagree with a refund decision:

  1. Request a review by contacting our management team
  2. We will conduct an independent internal review
  3. If unresolved, mediation through a neutral third party may be pursued
  4. You may lodge a complaint with the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner or seek legal advice

15. Special Circumstances

15.1 Wedding and Event Photography

Due to the time-sensitive, one-time nature of weddings and events:

  • Deposits are non-refundable once the date is secured
  • Cancellations within 60 days of event date result in full payment being due
  • We maintain backup equipment and backup photographer arrangements
  • If we cannot fulfill the booking, you receive full refund plus assistance finding alternative photographer

15.2 Commercial and Corporate Projects

Large-scale commercial projects may have custom refund terms negotiated in the service agreement. Where custom terms exist, they supersede this general policy.

16. Credit Notes and Future Services

In some circumstances, we may offer credit notes instead of refunds:

  • Credits can be used toward any of our services
  • Credits are valid for 12 months from issue date
  • Credits are non-transferable and have no cash value
  • Credit amounts may be discounted compared to cash refunds (typically 10-20% higher credit value)

17. Amendments to Refund Policy

We reserve the right to amend this Refund Policy at any time. Changes are effective immediately upon posting to our website. However, refund terms in effect at the time of booking govern that specific booking.

Current policy version can always be found at: https://saltframe.com/refund-policy/
