Frequently Asked Questions

What services does Salt & Frame offer?

We specialize in contemporary photography branding, visual identity design, brand strategy, professional photo sessions with natural lighting, and comprehensive visual storytelling packages tailored to your business needs.

How long does a typical branding project take?

Most comprehensive branding projects take 4-8 weeks from initial consultation to final delivery. Photography sessions can be scheduled within 1-2 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline during your consultation.

Do you work with clients outside Noeside?

Absolutely! While our studio is in Noeside, NT, we work with clients across Australia. We offer virtual consultations and can travel for on-location shoots when needed.

What makes your coastal-inspired aesthetic unique?

Our approach combines neutral tones with subtle oceanic undertones, creating warmth and authenticity. This coastal influence, paired with bright natural lighting and minimalist design, creates a distinctly Australian aesthetic that's both timeless and contemporary.

Can I schedule a consultation before committing?

Yes! We offer free initial consultations to discuss your vision, needs, and how our contemporary approach can elevate your brand. Contact us to schedule your consultation today.
